This category covers many a creamy delicacy, Some soups choose to elevate their status by using a dairy base to either a cheesy effect or a more creamy one. Cheesy soups often float broccoli or potatoes, while cream can afflict things like chicken, mushroom, or various vegetables.

A bowl of dairy based soup is often touted as more of a luxurious comfort than other soups. Or perhaps simply less healthy.
